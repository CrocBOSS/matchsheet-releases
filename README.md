## ✨ Features

- ⚽ **Soccer Match Tracking** - Complete match statistics and player performance
- 🏀 **Basketball Match Tracking** - Score tracking and player stats
- 💪 **Strength Training** - Track workout sessions and progress
- 🎯 **Technical Performance** - Skill-based training with scoring
- ⚡ **Speed Training** - Timer-based sprint tracking with analytics
- 📊 **Export Data** - Export training data to Excel and Text formats
- 🔄 **Auto-Updates** - Automatically notifies you of new versions
- 📱 **Offline First** - All data stored locally on your device

- Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or higher
- ~53 MB storage space

1. **Download** the APK from the [latest release](https://github.com/CrocBOSS/matchsheet-releases/releases/latest)

4. **Install** and enjoy!
